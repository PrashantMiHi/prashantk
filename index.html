§01 Point of View

AI is not a tool.
It is a supply chain.

Most organizations build isolated AI use cases. A chatbot here, an automation there. The pilots look impressive. The ROI is hard to find.

Real enterprise AI value comes from connecting the layers: data that is governed and AI-ready, knowledge that is versioned and entitlement-aware, platforms that standardize how agents are built and deployed, economics that make AI spend visible and accountable, and distribution that puts intelligence where decisions are made.

I work across this entire system — not as a theorist, but as the person who designs the architecture, builds the business case, aligns the stakeholders, and ships the platform.
